Become a RAIN-INN Ambassador

Represent RAIN-INN in your institution, coordinate local activities, and serve as the bridge between community members and RAIN headquarters.

The Role

What is a RAIN-INN Ambassador?

RAIN-INN Ambassadors are key representatives who bring the community to life at the institutional level. As an ambassador, you will:

  • Coordinate RAIN-INN activities and events at your institution
  • Serve as the primary communication link between members and RAIN headquarters
  • Help organize local cluster meetings focused on problem-solving
  • Promote community initiatives and recruit new members
  • Facilitate connections between stakeholders in your area

Requirements

Who Can Apply?

We're looking for passionate individuals from institutions across Nigeria and Africa who are committed to building the RAIN-INN community. You should have:

  • Active affiliation with a tertiary institution, company, government agency, or organization
  • Strong passion for AI, robotics, and technology-driven solutions
  • Demonstrated leadership experience in organizing events or managing teams
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Commitment to serving the community for at least one year
  • Basic familiarity with digital communication tools
