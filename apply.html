APPLICATION TO RAIN-INN

Eligibility Criteria

Are you an undergraduate in a tertiary institution with a strong enthusiasm for learning and contributing to the INnovators Network? Applications for the position of RAIN-INN Lead in your institution are now open. Don't miss the opportunity to apply before the deadline on March 15th, 2025. Ensure that you submit your application before the closing date. We welcome applications from passionate newcomers in tech and non-STEM students as well.

  • - Enrollment in a tertiary institution is a prerequisite.

  • - A minimum of one year until graduation is necessary.

  • - Demonstrate sustained passion and commitment over the course of a year.

  • - Showcase experience in team leadership and event planning.

  • - Possess familiarity with technical tools.

Benefits of being a Lead

  • - Access to specialized technical training sessions.

  • - Personal mentorship with experienced mentors affiliated with Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Nigeria.

  • - Opportunities for internships and collaboration through partnerships with RAIN.

  • - Exclusive networking opportunities with industry professionals.

  • - Exposure to cutting-edge technologies and trends in your field.

  • - Enhanced leadership and project management skills through hands-on experience.

  • - A chance to contribute actively to the INnovators Network community and shape its initiatives.

  • - Recognition and visibility as a leader within your institution and the broader tech community.
