Who We Are

Compassionate care,
exceptional medicine.

St Macrina Hospital Ltd is an established world-class private hospital located in the heart of Ibadan. We provide exceptional healthcare with state-of-the-art equipment and a team of skilled, warm-hearted professionals dedicated to your well-being.

From the warmth of our smiles to the precision of our procedures, every moment reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence and compassionate patient care.

What We Offer

Healthcare services for
every need.

🩺

General & Family Medicine

Comprehensive outpatient care for adults and families, including consultations, routine check-ups, and management of common illnesses.

🚨

Emergency & Urgent Care

24-hour emergency response for accidents, acute illnesses, and life-threatening conditions. Our team is always ready.

👶

Maternity & Obstetrics

Antenatal care, safe delivery services, and postnatal support by experienced midwives and obstetricians.

🧒

Paediatrics

Dedicated child health care from newborn check-ups to treatment of childhood illnesses with gentle, expert hands.

🔬

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Full in-house laboratory services for blood tests, cultures, and diagnostic screening with accurate, timely results.

💊

Pharmacy

On-site pharmacy dispensing prescribed medications and providing professional drug counseling for all patients.

🩸

Minor Surgery

Safe, sterile environment for minor surgical procedures performed by experienced medical practitioners.

Inpatient & Ward Care

Comfortable inpatient wards for patients requiring overnight monitoring, recovery, or extended treatment.

Preventive Healthcare

Health screenings, immunizations, and wellness programs designed to help you stay ahead of illness.
