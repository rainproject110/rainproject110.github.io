What Are Clusters?

Clusters are sector-specific silos within RAIN-INN where members from the same industry or field come together to discuss challenges, understand problems deeply, and identify AI and robotics solutions. When skill gaps are identified within a cluster, members can access RAIN's professional training and certification programs to build the capabilities needed to develop solutions.
