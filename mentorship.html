Join the Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Innovator Network as a

Are you a leader in Robotics and Artificial intelligence? Do you want to help shape the next generation of innovators? The Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Innovator Network is a vibrant community that promotes AI and robotics enthusiasts in advancing their skills through a collaborative peer-to-peer learning environment. We're excited to announce the launch of our volunteer-based mentorship program and invite experienced professionals to apply as mentors.

Why Become a Mentor?

As a mentor, you will play a pivotal role in guiding and inspiring upcoming AI/Robotics talents.

Impact knowledge

Share your knowledge and experience with motivated mentees.

Assist Mentees

Assist mentees in building their skills and confidence.

Foster Collaboration

Foster a collaborative learning environment.

Hackathon Challenge

Participate in the final hackathon, where mentees showcase their projects.

Community Contribution

Contribute to a community that values innovation, creativity, and teamwork.

Who We Are Looking For?

We welcome mentors with a background in AI/Robotics, excellent communication skills, and a collaborative spirit. Ideal candidates will have:

  • At least 2-3 years of experience in AI/Robotics or Innovative research & Product developmet.
  • A passion for mentoring.
  • A commitment to regular sessions, both online and offline.
  • A willingness to foster innovation and teamwork.
  • An interest in supporting a diverse and inclusive community.
  • A passion for connecting tech talents with industrial opportunities.

How to Apply

If you are interested in becoming a mentor, we encourage you to apply! The application process is simple:

  • Fill out our online application form
  • Provide details about your relevant experience and your approach to mentoring.
  • Share your availability for regular sessions




Join Us!

By joining our mentorship program, you'll be part of a dynamic community that thrives on collaboration and innovation. If you are ready to make a difference and guide the next generation of AI/Robotics professionals, we look forward to hearing from you! Duration- 6months & Renewable

Deadline for Applications: September 2024
