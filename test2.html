Our Story

Ibadan's favourite
gathering place.

Pleasure Summit is more than a restaurant — it's a destination. Nestled within the serene surroundings of Iyaganku GRA, PS offers exceptional Nigerian and intercontinental cuisine, chilled drinks, and vibrant entertainment all under one roof.

With over 10 kitchens each offering distinct specialities, guests enjoy a true food market experience alongside the refined comforts of a premium lounge bar.
